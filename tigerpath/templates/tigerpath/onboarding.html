Before we get started...

TigerPath is a tool, and is not a replacement for talking with your academic advisor. The official decision for whether or not a particular schedule fulfills your graduation requirements always rests with the university.

We recommend using TigerPath to plan out your schedule and then double-checking with your advisor to make sure that your schedule is reasonable and satisfies all of the requirements you want to satisfy.

TigerPath is currently in beta. If you find any bugs or have any suggestions, we would appreciate your feedback!

Prepopulate your course schedule (Optional)

If you're interested in prepopulating your four-year course schedule with courses you've already taken, then you can upload your unofficial transcript to the TigerApps Transcript Service.

Once you click on "Continue with Transcript Service" below, you will be redirected to the TigerApps Transcript Service webpage. Check "Share Courses", upload your transcript as a PDF file, and click the submit button. The service will extract the courses you've taken, and we'll automatically put them into your schedule.

Using the Transcript Service is optional. If you'd like, you can click "Finish" below and populate your schedule manually.
