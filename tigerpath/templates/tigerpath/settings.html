Prepopulate your course schedule

If you're interested in prepopulating your four-year course schedule with courses you've already taken, then you can upload your official transcript to the TigerApps Transcript Service.

Once you click on "Import Courses with Transcript Service" below, you will be redirected to the TigerApps Transcript Service webpage. Check "Share Courses", upload your official transcript as a PDF file, and click the submit button. The service will extract the courses you've taken, and we'll automatically put them into your schedule.

Import Courses with Transcript Service

Warning: This action cannot be undone! Importing courses will erase all of the courses that you currently have in your schedule.
